BIO-INTERACTIVE ROBOT DESIGN LAB

at Korea Institute of Science and Technology (KIST)

We foster bio-interactive intelligence by designing cutting-edge robot systems

​We aim to create intelligent robot systems at the intersection of architected materials, soft electronics, soft robotics, AI, and biomedical engineering. Our goal spans a broad range of future robot applications from cellular/tissue-scale microrobots to human-scale robotic skin and to environment-scale robotic construction.

Meet the Team!

We are a group of scientists, researchers, and students interested in topics ranging from metamaterials, soft electronics, soft robots, biomedical engineering, and AI
